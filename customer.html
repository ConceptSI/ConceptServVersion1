Emerging Chains

We are the experts

Concept Services specializes in assisting your new and upcoming chain in making sure that all your I’s are dotted, and your t’s are crossed. We are your turnkey equipment providers! From inception, our design experts will review your architectural plans to create a custom design package that will maximize the overall functionality of your kitchen, bar, bistro and market. Our custom design packages include a full MEP layout along with rough-in’s and elevations. No project is too big or too small! Once the design has been finalized and approved, our equipment specialists will provide an equipment quote that includes not only the equipment shown on your layout, but the accessories needed to complete your equipment package (gas hose kits, faucets, drains, etc.). Leave the small, but important details to us! While your site is being constructed, our team is working diligently to ensure that all your equipment is shipped, received and consolidated to a local warehouse to avoid any delays once the site is ready for delivery. We have installers nationwide that will fly out to your location and will complete a thorough inventory and inspection of all the equipment prior to delivery. This not only ensures a comprehensive delivery package but gives you a peace of mind that everything you’ve ordered will be on site and in mint condition. Once the equipment has been delivered and set-in-place, our team is ready to schedule and coordinate the startups of your cookline equipment, ice machines, ice cream machines, Woodstone ovens, walk-in cooler/freezers and more. We want your overall experience with Concept Services to be seamless and top notch. Our number one goal is to take all the worry, hassle and stress of building your upcoming chain and make the experience exciting, exceptional and worry free. Let us help you design, build and appreciate your vision!
